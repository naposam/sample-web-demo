ABOUT C.Y.E

Concern Youth Of Enmokanwo was established in November 29th, 2018 by a group of young people, led by Eric Nyamekye and Francis Aki Cobbinah; who could no longer accept the challenging situation in their community.

VISSION STATEMENT

C.Y.E is a youth association that is responsive to the changing needs of our youth and our community.

MISSION STATEMENT

C.Y.E is committed to promoting and facilitating youth attachment to society and community, connecting with organization and individuals with the view to support community development.

VALUE STATEMENT

In it's pursuit to improve development and quality of life through the causes it serves, C.Y.E is guided by the highest moral and ethical standards.

PRINCIPLES

  1. To operate with integrity, honesty truthfulness and conscience.
  2. To safe the public trust .
  3. Treat all people with dignity and respect.
  4. Value privacy and freedom of choice .
  5. To demonstrate concern for the interest of those affected by its action.

GOALS

  1. To create wide range of developmental opportunities for our youth and our community.
  2. To provide youth with skills and abilities to succeed in life.
  3. To provide a safe, supportive and fun environment for our youth.

OBJECTIVES

  1. To increase youth attachment to the association and the community.
  2. To provide opportunity for isolated youth to get involved in community activities.
  3. To promote public awareness of youth issues and needs.
  4. To reduce negative behaviour of our youth.
